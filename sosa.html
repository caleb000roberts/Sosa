WHO ARE WE?

Sosa Ministries Home was founded in the summer of 2022, just outside of Chicago, IL, by Pastor Cesar Bautista of 316 City Church. Since it's inception, the Sosa Ministries Home program has grown tremendously, helping the lost and the downtrodden find the light again, in themselves, and in Christ. Through the collaboration with 316 City Church, Sosa Ministries Home has helped countless people through guidance, community events, and most importantly spiritual support. We look forward to learning how we can help you find faith.

HOW DOES IT WORK?

SOSA Ministries Home is a Discipleship program, lasting from six months to one year, that we have created for individuals and families who are struggling with substance abuse, homelessness, and for those who have strayed away from Christ. During your stay with us, you will be provided room and board, as well as ongoing support to both rectify past wrongdoings, and to move forward. Participants will be will find spiritual support with us through 316 City Church. Contact us for more details!

OUR PROGRAM CONSISTS OF THREE PHASES

RESTORATION
During the first 3-4 weeks in our care, the participant leaves the Home only to attend church. The goal of this phase is for the participant to focus on themselves and what they truly want. This phase includes Detoxification, Cognitive Behavioral Therapy, and Trauma Counseling.
DISCIPLESHIP
Following the first 3-4 weeks, the Discipleship phase begins. This step includes working one on one with the Pastoral team, Bible Doctrine Training, and working with our Above and Beyond Counseling Center.
MENTORSHIP
The final phase of our process is the Mentoring phase. During this time the participant will receive continued guidance with our team as well as work placement, housing support, and educational resources as they transition out of the Home.
